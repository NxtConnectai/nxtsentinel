The 3-Second Defense

When you say your secret keyword (for example, "Red Weather"), Sentinel executes three critical actions simultaneously.

1. Hides Apps

Banking, photos, and social apps vanish instantly from your home screen and search. They cannot be found until you authenticate.

2. Alerts Help

A silent SMS with your exact GPS location is sent to your emergency contacts and (where supported) 911 services.

3. Locks Device

The device enters a "System Maintenance" lockdown mode. It can only be unlocked with a master password known only to you.
