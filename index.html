The 3-Second Defense

When you say your secret keyword (e.g., "Red Weather"), Sentinel executes three critical actions simultaneously.

1. Hides Apps

Banking, photos, and social apps vanish instantly from your home screen and search. They cannot be found until you authenticate.

2. Alerts Help

A silent SMS with your exact GPS location is sent to your emergency contacts and (where supported) 911 services.

3. Locks Device

The device enters a "System Maintenance" lockdown mode. It can only be unlocked with a master password known only to you.

On-Device. Encrypted. Private.

We built Sentinel with a "Zero Knowledge" architecture. We don't want your data, so we don't store it.

  • Voice processing happens 100% offline.
  • App lists and contacts never leave your phone.
  • Master password is stored in Hardware Keychain.
  • Works without an internet connection (SMS).
